## The One-Line Pitch

Stack62 is the AI-native operating system for small and mid-sized businesses — it lets any team build, run, and govern their own custom business software in minutes, without engineers.

---

## The Problem

Small and mid-sized businesses (SMBs) run on a patchwork of generic SaaS tools — CRMs, spreadsheets, approval chains, inventory trackers — that were never built for their workflows. Hiring developers to build custom software is expensive and slow. No-code tools require constant babysitting and break when requirements change.

The result: most SMBs operate on Excel, WhatsApp, and manual coordination. Every process is a fire drill. Every scaling event is a crisis.

---

## The Solution

Stack62 gives any business a **Coworker** — an AI agent that understands the business in plain language and builds the systems it needs. When a manager says "we need a procurement approval workflow with supplier tracking," Coworker drafts the system, shows a diff, and asks for approval before making anything live.

This **Plan → Diff → Approve** loop is the core of Stack62's trust model. Every AI-generated change is scored for risk, shown to a human, and requires explicit sign-off. The AI does the work. Humans stay in control.

Once a system is live, it runs natively on Stack62: real data records, automated workflows, document generation, reporting dashboards, and role-based access control — all with no separate tool.

## Traction & Differentiation

**Why Stack62 wins:**

1. **Systems run on Stack62** — not exported. This is the moat. Unlike tools that generate code and hand it back, Stack62 systems live in the platform, giving the business continuous AI assistance, versioning, and rollback.

2. **Governance-first AI** — Every change is diffed and approved. This is not optional. Regulated and cautious businesses (the majority of the SMB market) need this.

3. **Multi-provider AI** — Works with Anthropic, OpenRouter, and self-hosted Ollama. Businesses in data-sensitive industries can keep AI on-premise.

4. **Vertical-ready** — Pre-built system blueprints for SME operations (CRM + approvals + inventory), healthcare admin, legal case management, and more.
